UPstart - Feasibility Study

Introduction to the Project

UPstart is a web platform created by students for students of Pavol Jozef Šafárik University (UPJŠ). It serves as a digital guide for first-year students who often face many questions related to studies, schedules, etc.

Initial Status, Goal, and Project History

The project was created as a response to personal experiences of students who struggled to find basic information during their first year. The goal is to simplify adaptation and make the beginning of university life smoother and less stressful.

Market Analysis, Marketing Strategy, Target Groups

Market

  • 1000+ new students join UPJŠ each year.
  • Growth potential in case of expansion to other universities.

Marketing

  • Promotion via social media (Instagram, Facebook).
  • Cooperation with faculties and student organizations.
  • QR codes on posters during enrollment and welcome weeks.

Target Groups

  • First-year students at UPJŠ (main audience).
  • Tutors who can recommend the platform.
  • High school students considering studying at UPJŠ.

Financial Plan and Economic Analysis

  • Costs: domain & hosting (~€50/year), design/logos (~€0–50 if self-made).
  • Revenue (if monetized): ads, affiliate links, grants or UPJŠ support.

Project Implementation Plan

  • Website design and layout – completed
  • Content creation (FAQs, tips, maps) – on going.
  • Launch of beta version – [2026-2027].
  • Marketing – September (start of semester).
  • Feedback collection – October.

Material Inputs and Resources

  • Hosting and domain (Websupport / Forpsi).
  • CMS (WordPress, Webflow, or custom solution).
  • Content created voluntarily by students.

Technical and Technological Solution

  • Responsive website optimized for mobile devices.
  • Possibility to add interactive features (chatbot, event registration).
  • CMS system for easy content management and updates.

Risk Analysis

Risk Probability Impact Solution
Low traffic Medium Medium Strengthen marketing efforts
Outdated information High High Regular updates, feedback system
Technical issues Low Low Backups, testing, maintenance

Environmental Impact

The digital format helps reduce paper and print material usage, which is commonly distributed during enrollment and orientation. The project is environmentally friendly and sustainable.

Final Evaluation

UPstart is a low-cost project with high social impact and real value for new students. It has the potential to grow and become an official onboarding tool for first-year students at UPJŠ – and possibly at other universities in the future.
