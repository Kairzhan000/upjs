UPstart – Financial Analysis

Project Description

UPstart is an online platform and mobile application designed for first-year students at Pavol Jozef Šafárik University. The goal of the project is to help newcomers adapt more easily to university life by providing information about the city, university, housing, transportation, and student life.

Conclusion

The UPstart project is fully developed and managed by our four-member student team without any paid personnel. With initial and ongoing costs, the project operates with a profit of approximately €800 in the first year. UPstart provides strong social value and great potential for future expansion, offering first-year students essential guidance and support at UPJŠ.
